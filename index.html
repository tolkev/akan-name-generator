Akan Name Generator

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and thena and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan name.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequesntly in Ghana,children are given their first name 'as a day name' which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.
